ABOUT US

Gurucharan College, established on 15th July, 1935 is recognised as one of the premier institutions in the field of Collegiate education.
It has carved out a distinct identity of its own in the field of higher education in the eastern part of India.
The objective of this college is : ‘To strive, to seek, to find and not to yield’.
